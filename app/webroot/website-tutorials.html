How to Create a Splash News Article

  1. Go to your website’s homepage

  2. At the bottom of the homepage, click on the link named "Click Here to Login"

  3. Type in your username and password and then click the Login button

  4. In the menubar go to: Manage → News → New News

  5. Type out news article

  6. Choose a splash image (if available) in the Details panel

  7. Click on the "Featured on Homepage" checkbox in the Details panel

  8. Finally, click save button (at top right)

The new article should now appear on your website’s homepage
